KARMINE CORP X ORANGE

Scoring and Sound Design
JANUARY 2022

Karmine Corp x Orange

January 2022

Scoring and Sound Design for a video announcing Orange as a new sponsor for Karmine Corp.

It was a pleasure to work on this project alongside Loïc Goraguer who did the video work.

The audio was made on short notice within 3 days.

Read More

Wanted something that felt bold and determined as soon as the button was pressed. So I started with this idea by working on a bassline that could be reminiscent of Valorant’s soundtrack or even the Psyops theme from the League of Legends skin line.

#Process Video → Bassline & Tech

The bassline was made using multiple layers of u-He Diva, spatialized differently with a bunch of automation. Kicks and most of the other synths were made using u-He Zebra, sometimes rendered and reversed or cut in different ways to make it more organic.
Low/Midrange vox is a Zebra’s synth. Used my voice for the higher/more ‘breathy’ one.

#Process Video → Orchestral Ascension

Tried to compose something that could end on a VI VII i. Didn’t want it to sound too triumphant by playing a major I as the road to worlds is quite challenging.
Overall, the switch from a Dark Tech soundtrack to Orchestral really works well when the ascension starts, which is supposed to be more uplifting.
Added choirs to double down on the ‘epic’ challenge.
