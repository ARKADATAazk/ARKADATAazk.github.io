Viking Village

March 2021

Student project using the free Viking Village map from Unity’s assets to study Wwise.

This was a group project where we did the sound design and audio integration of a map to bring it to life. 

I was responsible for handling the Unity session (game design and audio systems programming) and acted as an audio and creative lead for a team of 7 sound designers.

The map was overhauled and multiple scripts were coded in order to achieve our creative vision and try out different systems.

  • Tools:

    Unity / Wwise / Reaper / C#
