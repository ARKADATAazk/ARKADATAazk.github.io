Pierre Daunis

Composer / Sound Designer

Music and Sound Design student at ISART Digital Paris, with a background in Motion Design and Video Editing. 

Since I was ten, I’ve been fascinated with audiovisual, but it’s only recently that I discovered a newfound love for the audio aspect. Not too long ago I was working freelance as a Motion Designer and Video Editor, but after working so long on differents media, always looking for the most fitting music. I felt like I should try my hand at composing and sound design.

Proud of that symbiotic skillset, I want to see how far audio and visual can be intertwined, especially in videogames where syncing events can be quite difficult due to the unpredictability of the human player. 

With a good understanding of programming and game engines, I intend to specialize in making deep and intricate audio systems to help enhance the player experience.
And if at some point in the process of this long adventure, I’m competent enough to live off of making and integrating videogame music, I’d welcome it.
